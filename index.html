Your Home in Kathmanduकाठमाडौंमा तपाईंको घर

Myagdi Alok Guest House is a warm, family-run guesthouse just 2 minutes from the New Bus Park. We offer comfortable rooms, delicious food, and a genuine Nepali welcome. Atithi Devo Bhava — the guest is our God. 🙏

म्याग्दी आलोक गेस्ट हाउस काठमाडौंको नयाँ बस पार्कबाट मात्र २ मिनेट टाढा। सफा कोठा, मिठो खाना र साँचो नेपाली स्वागत। अतिथी देवो भव 🙏
